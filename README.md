# 🤖 FinTerms AI

## Personal Finance Learning & Investment Assistant

FinTerms AI is a beginner-friendly personal finance platform designed to help users learn financial concepts, plan investments, assess risk, and track financial health through an interactive dashboard.

Built for the Microsoft Agents League Hackathon 2026.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📚 Finance Q&A

Learn common finance terms instantly:

* SIP
* NAV
* ETF
* CAGR
* Inflation

### 📈 SIP Calculator

Estimate future investment value based on:

* Monthly SIP amount
* Investment duration
* Compounding growth

### 🎯 Risk Profiler

Identify your investment personality:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

Receive investment suggestions based on risk appetite.

### 🤖 Finance Agent

Get AI-inspired investment recommendations based on:

* Age
* Monthly investment amount
* Risk profile

### 🎯 Goal Planner

Plan financial goals such as:

* Buying a car
* Purchasing a house
* Retirement planning

Calculate required monthly investments to achieve goals.

### 📊 Financial Dashboard

Track:

* Monthly SIP
* Financial Goals
* Risk Profile
* Projected Wealth
* Finance Score

### 📉 Finance Health Meter

Visual financial health indicator:

* Needs Improvement
* Good
* Excellent

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Many beginners struggle with financial planning due to unfamiliar terminology and lack of easy-to-use tools.

FinTerms AI simplifies personal finance by combining education, planning tools, and financial insights into a single platform.
